Introducing the Future of Visual Connection with Support for 360° Video & VR

SFERUS Connect360 is a platform for new, secure video-communication and real-time VR streaming using 360° video. Connect360 works with most popular 360° (VR) cameras, mobile and pc devices and browsers, and VR headsets. What scuba-diving is to space walks, Connect360 is to teleportation. Please be responsible when using a VR headset.

Next-gen

The next-generation platform supports massively-scalable conference and live-streaming sessions with independent, interactive 360° and VR viewing for all participants.

For Beginners and Pros

Anyone can easily connect via a pc or mobile browser, or with a VR headset. Setting up and sharing your own 360° sessions is just as simple and all you need is a supported VR-camera connected to a PC or mobile device.

Experience Reality in VR

Connect360 brings together the best of multi-user conferencing and VR real-time streaming, where one or several VR-cameras can be used within a single Connect360 session. The platform can be comfortably used for personal, day-to-day connections.

Business Use Cases

Unlike other conferencing platforms that either display 2D, non-immersive video or fully-virtual 3D conference environments, Connect360 can “teleport” participants to your location from anywhere in the world in VR, when you start or join a session using a standard and widely-available VR-camera. Connect360 is working together with our global partners to test novel areas of application.

Corporate communication

Corporate (formal) and personal (informal) group communication in 360°/VR

Multi-camera broadcasts, such as events, ceremonies, sports and virtual tourism

Educational engagement, webinars, virtual presence, examination real-time monitoring and recording

Real-estate

Remote property demonstrations and sales, inspections and audit of production facilities

Establishing a Connection

A Connect360 session can be started from a personal computer (Windows® or Mac®) with a VR-camera that supports a wired USB or HDMI connection for lowest latency.

The Connect360 Mobile App can recieve video from both a wireless and wired connection to a VR camera, in order to initiate or take part in a conference or start a real-time 360° live stream.

We offer custom AI and MV integration for Connect360 business customers and we’re also developing custom plug-ins for specialised VR cameras that posess onboard processing capabilities.

Connect360 cross-platform VR support allows anyone taking park to see the VR-camera point-of-view from anywhere in the world, while retaining the ability to communicate with other session participnats.
